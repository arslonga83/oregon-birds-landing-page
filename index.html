Locations

Oregon has an incredible variety of habitats within reach, from rocky shores to mountain summits, mossy forests, and high desert.

Oregon Coast

The rocky outcroppings in the Pacific Ocean make for incredible scenery, and are also home to many thriving seabird colonies. Visit here in the summer for the best chance to see Tufted Puffins.

High Desert

East of the Cascades, the land becomes dry, the sun intense, and the views endless. Here you can search the skies for Golden Eagles and the scour the sage brush for California Quail.

Mount Hood

In less the 2 hours from downtown Portland you can be walking on a glacier to look for Gray-crowned Rosy-finch and Ruffed Grouse.
